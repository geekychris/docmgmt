╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║  WHAT WAS FIXED                                          ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Added to application.properties:
  vaadin.excludeUrls=/api/**,/swagger-ui/**,/v3/api-docs/**

This tells Vaadin to NOT intercept paths starting with /api/,
allowing Spring MVC to handle Swagger UI properly.
